Our Team

Danish Aftab

Managing Partner

Mr. Aftab is licensed to practice in the High Courts of Pakistan. He holds an LL.B (Honours) from the University of London and an LL.M from the University of California, Berkeley with Certificates of Specialization in Business Law and Intellectual Property and Technology Law.

He is a Research Fellow to China Law Society and serves as an International Consultant to Kunming International Arbitration Service Center in Kunming, China.

Mr. Aftab has extensive litigation experience in constitutional, corporate, tax, intellectual property, and white-collar crime matters. He has served three Attorney-Generals for Pakistan and was part of the team that successfully settled the $1.2 Billion ICSID Award in the Karkey matter.
