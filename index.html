ABOUT US

Danish Legal Advocates and Corporate Counsels LLP is a full-service law firm headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan. We combine deep legal expertise with practical business understanding to deliver solutions that truly matter to our clients.

We serve clients across Pakistan with a strong focus on understanding local dynamics while maintaining international best practices. Our Islamabad office serves as the central hub of our operations, allowing us to effectively serve both national and international clients.

Our Practice Areas

Corporate & Commercial Law

Company formation, contracts, mergers, compliance, shareholders agreements, and corporate governance.

Family Law

Marriage, divorce, khula, child custody, maintenance, inheritance, and family disputes.

Real Estate & Property Law

Property transactions, title searches, rent disputes, construction agreements, and land acquisition.

Criminal Defense

Defense in criminal cases, bail applications, FIR quashing, and white-collar crime cases.

Banking & Finance Law

Banking disputes, loan recovery, financial regulations, and debt recovery matters.

Taxation Law

Income tax, sales tax, federal excise, tax planning, and tax litigation.

Intellectual Property Law

Trademark registration, copyright protection, patents, and IP enforcement.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Civil litigation, commercial disputes, arbitration, mediation, and alternative dispute resolution.
