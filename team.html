Our Team

Danish Aftab

Managing Partner

Mr. Aftab is licensed to practice in the High Courts of Pakistan. He has completed his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B Honours) from the University of London. He also holds a Master of Laws (LL.M) from the University of California, Berkeley (USA) with Certificates of Specialization in Business Law and Intellectual Property and Technology Law from Berkeley Law School.

Mr. Aftab is also the Research Fellow to China Law Society and is serving as an International Consultant to Kunming International Arbitration Service Center in Kunming, China.

Mr. Aftab has extensively practised and litigated before the High Court(s) of Pakistan in matters relating to constitutional law, corporate law, tax law, family law, intellectual property law, privacy law, defamation law, property law, civil cases, criminal cases and white collar crime cases.

Mr. Aftab has served three Hon'ble Attorney-Generals for Pakistan: Mr. Ashtar Ausaf Ali, Mr. Khalid Javaid Khan and Mr. Anwar Mansoor Khan. He has worked as an Associate to the Office of the Attorney General for Pakistan, assisting the Attorney-General for Pakistan with legal research and drafting.

Mr. Aftab served as a member of the negotiations team that amicably settled the $1.2 Billion ICSID Award in the Karkey matter against Pakistan.

Mr. Aftab has served as counsel in negotiations and settlement talks for Pakistan’s International Disputes. He served as counsel for Pakistan's International Arbitration disputes.

Mr. Aftab worked on cases, prepared legal memoranda, and drafted briefs to assist the Attorney-General for Pakistan before the Supreme Court on constitutional matters pertaining to dual nationality of civil servants, contempt of court, the disqualification of former Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif, judicial accountability and judicial activism among others.

Mr. Aftab worked with the Ministry of Law and Justice on proposing changes to outdated legislations.

Mr. Aftab also regularly appears and argues as counsel before the High Courts, District Courts and various other Tribunals. He has extensive experience in researching case law, preparing legal briefs and memoranda, as well as in drafting writ petitions, civil revisions, civil appeals, review petitions, complaints to regulators, plaints, replies to petitions and applications, conducting/preparing examination in chief and cross-examinations.

Mr. Aftab also renders legal advice to companies over matters regarding registration of companies and trade associations. He has also dealt with matters pertaining to white collar crimes before the Accountability Court.
